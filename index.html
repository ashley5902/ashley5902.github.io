Your Guide to the Fashion World

Trending Fashion Styles

Current trending fashion styles include wide legged pants and long flowy skirts. Out are skinny jeans as they are seen as millenial clothing. Cropped jackets are also making a comeback due to its wonderful pairing with wide leg and high rise denim jeans. Suit vests and sweater vests are perfect to adorn in the fall-like season and are typically worn with long skirts. Stripes are still in and perfect for every body type. As for colors, red is the hot pick of the current season for a nice pop. Pointed toe flats and slingback kitten heels are all the rage and pair perfectly with heans for a simple "elegance" look for the day into night.

High Couture Fashion Designers

High couture fashion designers include Donatella Versace, Giorgio Armai and Bouchra Jarrar. Additional notable designers include Coco Chanel, Christian Dior, Mario Valentino, and Karl Lagerfeld.
